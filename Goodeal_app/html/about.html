About us

As a result of the covid-19, many businesses closed. Many others had to rethink teleworking. As a consequence, many businesses have rethought their business model and have realized that according to what type of work it is much more profitable to do it from home.

That is why we have found this niche and we have filled it, since until now there was no app to search for telework.

What differentiates us from the others Apps?

The main difference is that our job offers are limited to teleworking, but with "normal" contracts as if you were going to work on site. For this reason, we want to take the best of being a freelance worker, to have independence and autonomy, but the security and stability of a salaried worker.
